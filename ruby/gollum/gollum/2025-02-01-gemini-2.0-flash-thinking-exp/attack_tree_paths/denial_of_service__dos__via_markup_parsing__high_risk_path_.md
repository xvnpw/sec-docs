### 1. Define Objective

The objective of this deep analysis is to thoroughly investigate the "Denial of Service (DoS) via Markup Parsing" attack path in the context of Gollum. This includes:

*   Understanding the technical details of how this attack can be executed against Gollum.
*   Identifying potential vulnerabilities within Gollum's markup parsing process that could be exploited.
*   Evaluating the severity and impact of a successful DoS attack via markup parsing.
*   Analyzing the effectiveness of the proposed mitigations and suggesting further improvements or alternative strategies.
*   Providing actionable recommendations for the development team to enhance the security and resilience of the Gollum application against this specific attack vector.

### 2. Scope

This analysis focuses specifically on the "Denial of Service (DoS) via Markup Parsing" attack path. The scope includes:

*   **Attack Vector Analysis:** Detailed examination of how malicious markup can be crafted and injected into Gollum.

*   **Impact Assessment:** Evaluation of the consequences of a successful DoS attack on the Gollum application and its users.
*   **Mitigation Strategy Evaluation:**  Critical review of the proposed mitigation measures and exploration of additional security controls.
*   **Gollum Context:**  Analysis is specifically tailored to the Gollum wiki application and its potential vulnerabilities related to markup parsing.

This analysis will not cover other DoS attack vectors against Gollum, such as network-level attacks or application logic flaws unrelated to markup parsing.

4.  **Mitigation Analysis:**  Evaluating the effectiveness of each proposed mitigation strategy in the context of Gollum. This includes considering the feasibility of implementation, potential performance impact, and overall security benefit.
5.  **Best Practices Review:**  Referencing industry best practices for secure markup parsing and DoS prevention to identify additional mitigation measures and recommendations.
6.  **Documentation and Reporting:**  Compiling the findings into this detailed analysis document, providing clear explanations, actionable recommendations, and prioritizing mitigation efforts based on risk.
